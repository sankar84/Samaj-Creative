About Us

The Malayalee samajam madurai is affiliated to the "Confederation of Tamil Nadu Malayalee Association (CTMA), an organisation in Tamil Nadu aiming to project and present the ethnic diversity of Malayalees. It functions as a common platform for the manifold ideas and plans within the framework of legal and cultural interest with the socially at large.

There are two categories of Members : Life Members & Annual Members

Annual Members have to pay a fee of Rs. 150/- every year. Life Members have to pay a lifetime fee of Rs. 1000/-

Any one who is a Malayalee by parently and above 18 years of age residing in Madurai and Suburbs is eligble for Membership. All members are expected to follow the rules and regulations of the Samajam.

What we do

The Madurai Malayalee Association is a secular, non-political, non-partisan, and an incorporated non-profit organization of Keralites in Madurai.

To promote social and cultural integration of Malayalees in Madurai.

To offer assistance and guidance to the Members.

To provide help and guide the needy.

To promote advancement of education by providing scholarships, bursaries and prizes for scholastic achievement.
